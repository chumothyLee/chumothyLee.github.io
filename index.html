My Story

  • Pre-2014

    My Home, The City of Angels

    I'm a Los Angeles native and take a lot of pride in that. The food is great, the people are interesting, and it never gets boring. LA will always be the place I call home.

  • 2014

    Moving to Sunny San Diaaago

    I chose to go to UC Socially Dea... I mean... UC San Diego for college. I majored in Computer Science for reasons unknown to me at the time, but the more I learned, the more I loved (and hated) it. I had fun meeting some super smart people, learning (just a little), and exploring a new city. Everyday was different and it didn't hurt that the weather was always perfect.

  • Late 2015 - Early 2017

    A Kinda-Sorta-Internship

    All my friends were starting to look for internships so I figured I would give it a shot too. This led me to MorningSignOut, an extremely young startup. I did some iOS development there during my sophomore and junior years. Our first app was released to the Apple App Store. It's called SilverLiningsMSO and its just ok.

  • Summer of 2017

    Its a bird. Its a plane. Its a ViaSat satellite.

    Following the end of my third year, I interned at ViaSat. It was super fun. I learned a lot, particularly about inexperienced I was and also started to become interested in design. I was assigned to a cool stand-alone project, learned a lot of neat things, and met some really intelligent people.

  • 2018

    "This isn't even my final form"

    I graduate from UC San Diego this year! I'm currently looking for full-time developer positions but am also considering pursuing a master's degree in Human Computer Interaction. I'm not sure where I'm gonna end up but I'm definitely excited at the possibilities.
